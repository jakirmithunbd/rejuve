Hangover IV Drip Ingredients

Here is how each ingredient in the Hangover IV works to give you the boost and relief that you're craving.

B12

Base ingredient

Cobalamin (B12) supports the normal functioning of your nerve cells. It gives your body a boost of energy that helps to improve memory and concentration. It also elevates your mood by providing your body with serotonin.

Calcium Chloride

Base ingredient

Calcium is the most common cation and the fifth most abundant inorganic element in the human body. It is essential for the maintenance of the nervous, muscular, and skeletal systems and cell membrane and capillary permeability. Calcium's role in building bones and supporting their health is well known. Still, it is also essential for nerve conduction, blood coagulation, and electrical conduction in the heart's muscular tissue.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are the answers to some of the most common questions we hear from our appreciated customers.

Is IV Therapy Safe?

Yes, we only use reputable and accredited compounding pharmacies for our ingredients. These infusions are administered by licensed skilled IV specialists.

Are there side effects?

Side effects rarely happen if at all. If they do they are usually minor and short lived. Bruising or bleeding at IV site, warming or cooling sensation in arm, chest or stomach, tasting the vitamins or having vitamin smell in your urine. Our licensed staff are trained to manage all side effects that may occur.

Does IV Treatment hurt?

We use only skilled professionals for IV placement to ensure the least amount of pain is felt if any at all. A numbing topical cream can be purchased for use to dull the sensation at request. Once the IV is in place only a very small straw will be left in the vein ensuring a comfortable session.
